Our Mission

Here at Spiritual Journey our mission is to be a valuable resource along someone’s spiritual journey towards enlightenment. Whether you are a complete beginner, 10 year experienced practitioner or an enlightened master there is something for you to learn on this website.

Our goal is simply to be a guide and companion on anyone’s spiritual journey. We gather the most vital information, filter the wheat from the chaff and deliver you the most important advice to reach your goal of awakening. We take a strict approach of only teaching material that directly leads to enlightenment because on the spiritual journey there are a lot of tangents one can take, but we try to keep you on track to the big ticket which is enlightenment. We also support those who are enlightened and are confused on what to do now. There are countless resources teaching you how to get to enlightenment but few who tell you how to integrate it after the journey has taken place.

We are a non-profit organisation and are not trying to sell or convert you to any belief. We simply lay out the information and it is up to you to interpret this information. Spend the time to look around the website and simply enjoy the journey.
