Meditation Retreats

Wherever your location, there is probably a meditation retreat nearby. Dhamma.org has locations in most countries where you can go on a 10 day retreat or silence. In these retreats you go through various sitting, standing and walking meditations and spend your 10 days in complete silence. These retreats can enhance your spiritual work tenfold and serious self-knowledge can be achieved
